Male, US, born 01/28/1895, died 06/08/1982…  Wallace Neff was born in La Mirada, CA. He lived at 1883 Orlando Street in San Marino, CA, in 1930, in a residence that had an approximate value of $100,000, a large sum for the period. A prolific and respected architect whose work is seen throughout San Marino. He died in Laguna Beach, CA, at the age of 87.
