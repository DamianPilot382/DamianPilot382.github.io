Gordon Bernie Kaufmann was an English-born American architect mostly known for his work on the Hoover Dam.  He was born in 1888 in the UK and died in 1949 in LA.  Along with work in San Marino at 1640 Oak Grove (Millikan House, 1929, designed for House of a CalTech physics professor, one of the giants in his field) and 1780 Lombardy, he also worked on Santa Anita Park.
